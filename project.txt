Project: Task Tracker

Purpose:
The Task Tracker is a streamlined application designed to help users efficiently manage tasks,
prioritize activities, and stay organized. 

Features:
Task Management: Easily add, edit, delete, and mark tasks as completed or pending.

Categorization & Prioritization: Organize tasks into categories and set priorities (High, Medium, Low).

Deadline Tracking: Assign deadlines and receive reminders for upcoming or overdue tasks.

Search & Filter: Quickly search or filter tasks by name, category, status, or priority.

Progress Tracking: View overall progress through visual indicators like completion percentages.
